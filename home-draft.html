--- title: MatchMaker Education Labs date: 2019-11-05 ---

MatchMaker Education Labs™ enables the smart cataloging of a dynamic registry of learning competencies, curriculum and content powering learning search of educational resources, PreK to Grey.

In much the same way that Pandora's Music Genome Project transformed the music industry, MatchMaker's Registry will revolutionize the learning industry by dynamically associating rich relationships between educational elements to truly liberate learning.

MatchMaker K12, the MMEdLabs’ B2B SaaS offering, introduces the Labs' Smart Cataloging Platform, Dynamic Registry, and ground-breaking Learning Search Technology for the K12 market. Facilitating more effective design, discovery, and use of learnign resources, MatchMaker Ed Labs at long last facilitates the transformation of static, one-size-fits-all curriculum into dynamic, learner-centered education.

Everyone Benefits from the Result

Our business partners can be confident that their products will be found by their customers and their products meet their customers’ needs. At long last, the educators, can find the learning resources they need when they need them!

One-Time Entry

 Assets need only to be described once to be included in the MMK12 registry.

Standardized Format

 Technical barriers to an ever-expanding registry are virtually eliminated because instructional resource data from all content providers are included in a standards-based format in MMK12’s registry of learning resources.

Easy Implementation

 Built on existing data standards, MMK12 is a turnkey solution that can integrate with existing infrastructure.

Automatic Updates

 New resource references and standards automatically relate to educator and learner needs. Therefore, no additional work is required when guiding documents are updated.

Seamless Integration

 MMK12’s SaaS model means that learning platforms can integrate the registry through a set of simple APIs. MMK12 customers can easily generate a custom catalog by subscribing to the MMK12 registry of commercial and open educational resources.

Multiple Media Repository

 Unlike some databases, which may be limited to one type of educational resource, MMK12’s registry includes information about any kind of resource – print, digital, video, physical, or online.
